Why does this happen? Essentially because electromagnetic waves kick $\theta$ around proportionally to its coupling to photons, which vanishes for spatially constant $\theta_1 = \theta_2 = 0$ (because $\vec \Theta$ then goes to constant $\Theta_3 = 1$ for any value of $\theta_3$, and the photon mass terms are derivatives in $\vec \Theta$). The EFT dynamics therefore drives $\theta$ to $\theta_3$ (note that this starts well before 32 lattice time units, so there is no question about it being a real physical effect):

In contrast to the plain NLSM case, the universe now becomes increasingly transparent as time goes by!
